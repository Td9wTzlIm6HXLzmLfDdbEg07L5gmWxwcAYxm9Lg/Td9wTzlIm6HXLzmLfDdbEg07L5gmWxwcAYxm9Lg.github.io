What is Delta Math?

Delta Math is a game hub disguised as a homework helper. It's a collection of the best browser games — action, racing, sports, puzzles, idle games, and more — all playable directly in your browser with no installs, no accounts, and no nonsense.

Everything runs in your tab. Just click and play.

Why is it "unblocked"?

Most school networks block gaming sites — but they can't block everything. Delta Math is designed to fly under the radar so you can get your game on without the IT department ruining your day.

That said: don't be dumb about it. Pay attention in class, do your work, and use this during lunch or free periods. This site isn't responsible for your grades. That's on you.

What we can't add

People ask all the time for games like Fortnite, NBA 2K, Call of Duty, Roblox, and similar titles. Here's the thing — those games are massive native applications. They can't run in a browser tab.

What we can add: browser-based games, HTML5 games, and embedded titles. Keep the suggestions coming and we'll keep adding what's actually possible.

How to suggest a game

Got a game you want added? Send it over. If it runs in a browser, there's a good chance we can get it on here. If it's a native app, it's not happening — but we appreciate the thought.

  • Has to be playable in a browser tab
  • Has to actually be fun (obvious, but still)
  • Not already on the site (check first)
  • Nothing that would get the site shut down immediately
